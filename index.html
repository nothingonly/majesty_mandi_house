Our Story

Experience Authentic Arabian Dining

Welcome to Majesty Mandi House, where we bring the rich culinary heritage of the Middle East straight to your plate in Greater Warangal. We specialize in authentic Arabian-style mandi preparations, crafted with love and tradition.

Our signature dishes feature fragrant basmati rice slow-cooked with aromatic spices, paired with incredibly tender, fall-off-the-bone meat. Served on large sharing platters, our meals are designed to bring people together.

Whether you're visiting with family, gathering with friends, or looking for a comfortable place to enjoy a hearty meal, our warm hospitality and family-friendly dining atmosphere will make you feel right at home.
